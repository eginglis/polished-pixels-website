Photography site case study

Case study of how we increased enquiries for this Perth based photography business 

In 2011 we built the website of a successful Perth based photographer, which showcased her beautiful photos and increased her enquiries. This photography website had some very amazing photos within it. The website owner is a very talented professional photographer. 

About the client

The goal of this website was to showcase her talents and also to increase sales for her small business. We helped her achieve this by giving her a professional online presence and also by helping her website to appear within search results by using Search Engine Optimisation techniques.

Quick facts

  • Client Stef Photography
  • Sector Photography industry
  • Start date 2011
  • Project length 1 month

What we did

  • Web and mobile design
  • Website maintenance and support
  • Search engine marketing
  • User-friendly web design

Stats and facts

  • Page views 6% increase
  • Time on site 25% increase
  • Bounce rate 10% decrease

It was our job to SEO the website or in other words make the website friendly to search engines. As a direct result of our SEO work, the website is ranking very highly in Google's search results.

Our client Stef Photography wanted to be found for search terms that are relevant to their business. If someone searches for any of the following relevant words using the Australian Google search engine, our client Stef Photography is at the very top of the search results!

  • 'portrait photos lancelin'
  • 'lancelin photographer'
  • 'portrait photographer lancelin '
  • 'photography in lancelin'
  • 'landscape photography lancelin'
