Business school website rebuild

Polished Pixel rebuilt Australia’s no 1 business school website

In 2016 MGSM was once again ranked the number one School in Australia by the Financial Times world MBA ranking. Polished Pixels rebuilt their website from the ground up, made the website mobile friendly and did an extensive amount of person centred design on this website.

Outcomes we achieved

  • Pages views 6% increase
  • Time on site 25% increase
  • Bounce rate 10% decrease

Quick facts

  • Client MGSM
  • Sector Higher Education
  • Start date August 2015
  • Project length 6 months

What we did

  • website strategy
  • project management
  • content audit
  • project planning / management
  • persona development
  • usability research
  • website rebuild using Squiz CMS (MySource Matrix)
